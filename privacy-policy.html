Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2026 | Effective Date: March 25, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how EJE ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with our social media accounts on Instagram, X (Twitter), TikTok, LinkedIn, and YouTube.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently. This policy applies to all data collected through direct messages (DMs), public interactions, and engagement with our content.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Direct Messages: Message content sent to our accounts (PII such as emails, phone numbers, and addresses is redacted at the point of collection and never stored in raw form)
  • Sender Identifiers: Platform-specific user IDs and usernames
  • Collaboration Proposals: Business inquiries sent via DM

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Engagement Metrics: Likes, comments, shares, saves on our content
  • Analytics Data: Impressions, reach, follower counts, engagement rates (aggregated)
  • Platform Metadata: Timestamps, platform identifiers for message routing
  • TikTok Account Information: Username, avatar URL, and posting capabilities when you connect your TikTok account
  • Publishing Metadata: Privacy settings, interaction preferences, and content disclosure selections made during publishing

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Financial information (credit card numbers, bank details)
  • Government-issued identification numbers
  • Biometric data
  • Location data beyond what platforms provide in analytics
  • Data from minors (we do not knowingly collect data from users under 13)

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access — You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Deletion — You may request that we delete your personal data. See Section 6.
  • Right to Correction — You may request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing — You may request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability — You may request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object — You may object to our processing of your personal data.

6. Data Deletion Process

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send a DM to any of our social media accounts requesting data deletion
  2. Our system will acknowledge your request with a reference ID (format: DEL-XXXXXXXXX)
  3. Your request is logged and our team is notified immediately
  4. We will process your request within 30 days of receipt

What Happens After You Request Deletion

  • All DM conversation records associated with your account are deleted
  • Your sender ID is removed from our CRM and lead databases
  • Collaboration records containing your information are purged
  • Aggregated analytics data (which cannot identify you) is retained
  • You will receive confirmation once deletion is complete

Exceptions: We may retain certain data if required by law, to resolve disputes, or to enforce our agreements. In such cases, we will notify you of the specific data retained and the legal basis.

7. California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the CCPA and CPRA:

  • Right to Know — See Sections 2 and 3 above.
  • Right to Delete — See Section 6.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale — We do not sell your personal information.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA/CPRA rights.

For CCPA/CPRA-specific requests, please include "CCPA Request" in your message.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • PII (emails, phone numbers, addresses) is redacted at the point of collection
  • Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized team members
  • Service endpoints are authenticated with secure tokens
  • Regular security audits are conducted on our systems
